Our Philosophy

Hug The Mug is more than a café. Is is a place for your mind and soul. We believe that the perfect cup of coffee is the companion to a great book or a sweet conversation. 

With daily baked pastries and shelves full of hand-picked literary gems, we design our space to be your second home. A quiet corner for remote work, a spot for reading or a cozy area for group activities.

FAQ

Can I bring my dog with me?

Of course! We love our fourlegged friends.

Can I bring my laptop or tablet?

You have work, you are tired of staying inside your place. We know how it is, that is why here at HugTheMug you can bring your laptop or tablet. However, we kindly ask you not to take a bigger table if you are alone.

Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?

Yes, we offer plant-based options.

Can I bring my reusable cup?

Yes and we encourage you to do it! Customers who do so, receive a small discount.
